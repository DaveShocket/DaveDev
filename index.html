Happy Birthday To Me!

July 7th

Dear Self,

Wishing myself a very happy birthday! Another year older, another year wiser. May this special day bring me lots of happiness, love, and all that my heart desires.

Load, as I celebrate another year of life that You have graciously given me, I come before You with a heart full of gratitude. Thank You for the precious gift of life and for all the blessings You have bestowed upon me throughout the years.

On this day, I ask for Your continued guidance and grace. May Your light shine brightly upon me, illuminating my path and filling my heart with peace and joy. Help me to walk in Your ways, to live according to Your will, and to serve others with love and compassion.

Thank You, Lord, for the gift of family and friends who support and love me. Bless them abundantly for their kindness and presence in my life.

On this birthday, I pray for health, happiness, and prosperity. May Your blessings overflow in my life and may I be a blessing to others.

In Your precious name, I pray.

Amen.


Celebrating myself and all that I've achieved!

"The more you praise and celebrate your life, the more there is in life to celebrate."
- Oprah Winfrey

Thank you for being awesome!
